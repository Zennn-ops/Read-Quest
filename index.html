HOW TO PLAY
📖 ReadQuest: The Lost Library
🧙‍♂️
MOVE YOUR HERO
Use Arrow Keys or WASD on keyboard. On mobile, tap the D-Pad buttons on screen to walk around the dungeon.
🟢
FIGHT ENEMIES
Walk into an enemy to battle it! A story appears — read it carefully, then pick the correct answer to defeat the enemy.
🔒
UNLOCK DOORS
Every locked door has a scroll or book to read. Answer the question correctly to open the door and move forward!
🪜
FIND THE STAIRS
After defeating all enemies and opening the door, stairs appear. Walk to them to go to the next room!
🎁
OPEN CHESTS
Some rooms have treasure chests 🎁. Walk into one to open it — it restores 1 heart ❤️ of your health!
❤️
WATCH YOUR HP
You start with 3 hearts. Wrong answers cost you 1 heart. If all hearts are gone — Game Over! Read carefully!
💡 PRO TIP
Read the whole passage before looking at the choices.
The answer is always hidden somewhere in the text!
